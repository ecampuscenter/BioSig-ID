If you are experiencing difficulty in creating or using your BioSig-ID™ or Click-ID™ passwords:

  • Review the FAQs below
  • If the FAQs do not address or solve the problem:
    • Request assistance from the BioSig-ID™ Help Desk by submitting a Help Ticket
    • Provide your Boise State email address and a screen shot of any error messages
  • If the Help Desk advice does not address or solve the problem:
    • Reply to the email and include your phone number and the best times to reach you

From 8 a.m. to 8 p.m. MST: Allow up to four hours for a response by email during the week, six hours during the weekend.
