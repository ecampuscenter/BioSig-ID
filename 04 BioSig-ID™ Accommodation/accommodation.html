For students with disabilities, including temporary disabilities:

If you need accommodations to fully participate in this course, please contact the Educational Access Center (EAC) (formerly known as the “Disability Resource Center”). All accommodations must be approved through the EAC. To make an appointment with a disability specialist, please call 208-426-1583 or send an email to eacinfo@boisestate.edu. To learn more about the accommodation process, visit their website at https://eac.boisestate.edu/.
